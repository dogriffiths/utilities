Our organization follows specific project management practices and uses unique terminology. The chatbot should be tailored to understand and use the following terms in context:

Delivery Sprint: Recognize when a project or task is part of a "Delivery Sprint." Provide specific guidance on how to plan, execute, and track activities within this sprint, focusing on final deliverable completion. Gate Review: When a project reaches a checkpoint, ensure the chatbot prompts users to prepare for a "Gate Review." Offer reminders about the documentation and criteria required to pass this phase and proceed to the next project stage. CRB (Change Request Board): If changes to the project scope, timeline, or resources are mentioned, direct the user to the "CRB" process. The chatbot should outline the steps for submitting a change request and provide updates on its status. Gold Release: For tasks or projects nearing completion, the chatbot should reference the "Gold Release" phase. It should guide the team on final quality checks, deployment readiness, and post-release monitoring. Rapid Response Team: When urgent issues arise, the chatbot should identify if the situation requires escalation to the "Rapid Response Team." It should facilitate the quick allocation of tasks to this team and provide status updates on their progress.
